Alexander Graham Bell

inventing the first practical telephone in 1876. Bell was a Scottish-born inventor
and scientist who was working on ways to improve telegraph communication. While
experimenting with transmitting sound over wires, he came up with the idea of using
a membrane that would vibrate in response to sound waves and transmit those vibrations
as electrical signals.

THE FIRST PHONE

telecommunications device that permits two or
